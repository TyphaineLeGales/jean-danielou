How can the challenges of the climate crisis be portrayed in works of fiction? How can we achieve an accurate representation of scientific activity in a world mired in post-truth?



The Nouvelle Séquence collective of screenwriters has developed a toolbox on scientific work to avoid clichés and help fiction more closely reflect scientific practice.

Toolbox is here : https://nouvelle-sequence-boite-a-outils-scientifiques.framer.website/
