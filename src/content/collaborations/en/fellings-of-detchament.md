\_\_\_\_Oil, tea, sugar, tobacco, wood, orchids… 

How should we look at globalization? How can we learn to see the global chains that put to work, discipline, and circulate around the world the bodies we mass consume ? How can we go beyond the smooth, shiny surface of the finished products that capitalism offers us on its shelves to trace the labyrinthine circuits that encircle the Earth and were our lives substances are formed ?
