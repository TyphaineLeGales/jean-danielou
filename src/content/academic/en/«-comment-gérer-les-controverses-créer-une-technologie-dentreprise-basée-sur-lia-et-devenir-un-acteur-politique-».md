title: « How to manage controversies? Making an AI-based corporate technology,
  becoming a political player »
date: "2026"
link: https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/abs/10.1002/9781394236855.ch21
publishedon: Stephens Keri & Faust Kasey (eds) The Handbook of Infrastructure
  Communication, Wiley
text: This chapter explores how an energy company develops and implements
  innovative solutions to better understand the controversies it faces by
  deciphering massive amounts of digital traces with natural language processing
  (NLP) technologies. These new tools aim at reframing the communication process
  between corporate entities and stakeholders, leading to major transformations
  in the way corporate entities assume a political role when delivering the
  infrastructures of the energy transition.
