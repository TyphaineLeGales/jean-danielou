title: Paving the way. The role of brokers in the territorialization of
  multinational corporations
date: "2024"
link: https://journals.openedition.org/rac/36609
publishedon: Revue d’anthropologie des connaissances
text: This article looks at the role played by a category of actors –the
  brokers- in the expansion of multinationals. Frequently invisible, their
  actions are nonetheless crucial in establishing the global connections that
  enable multinationals to grow and prosper around the world. With this figure,
  we propose to study the groundwork on which these global connections are
  built. Our investigation is based on the ethnography of a renewable energy
  infrastructure development project on Lubang Island in the Philippines. We
  show that the role of the broker is not only to connect people who are
  strangers to each other, but also and above all to prepare the ground for the
  territorialization of multinationals.
